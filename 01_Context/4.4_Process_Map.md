## Core Operational Workflow

The core operational workflow of SynTemp Vision is defined as follows:

1. Customer Order / Request  
   - Customer requirements are received and reviewed for completeness and clarity.

2. Work Order Creation  
   - A controlled work order is generated to define product specifications and requirements.

3. Material Selection & Procurement  
   - Required materials are selected or procured.  
   - Materials are verified for suitability prior to use.

4. Manufacturing / Fabrication  
   - Components are manufactured in accordance with work order requirements.

5. Assembly  
   - Components are assembled into finished instrumentation.

6. Testing / Calibration / Verification  
   - Products are tested or calibrated against defined acceptance criteria.

7. Inspection  
   - Final inspection verifies conformity to customer and internal requirements.

8. Release & Shipment  
   - Only approved products are released and shipped to the customer.

---

## Process Interaction

Customer requirements initiate the process.  
Each process step produces outputs that serve as inputs to the next step.  
Verification and inspection activities provide control points to ensure conformity before release.  

Supporting processes including document control, supplier control, risk management, internal audit, and corrective action support the effectiveness of the QMS.

---

## Monitoring

Process performance and effectiveness are reviewed through work order records, inspection results, nonconformance data, and Management Review.
