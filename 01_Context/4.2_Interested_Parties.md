# 4.2 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

Owner:     
Approved By:   
Effective Date: 2026-03-01  

---

## Purpose

To identify interested parties relevant to the Quality Management System (QMS) and understand their requirements.

---

## Interested Parties and Requirements

SynTemp Vision has identified the following interested parties:

| Interested Party | Relevant Requirements |
|------------------|----------------------|
| Customers | Product performance, accuracy, on-time delivery, compliance with specifications |
| Suppliers | Clear specifications, communication, defined purchasing requirements |
| Employees | Defined roles, training, safe work environment |
| Regulatory Bodies | Compliance with applicable regulations |
| Certification Body | Conformance to ISO 9001 |
| Ownership / Management | Business performance and risk control |

---

## Review

Interested parties and their requirements are reviewed during Management Review or when significant business changes occur.

---

## Responsibility

Top Management is responsible for ensuring that relevant interested parties and their requirements are considered within the QMS.
