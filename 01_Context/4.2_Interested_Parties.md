# 4.2 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

## Purpose
To identify interested parties relevant to the QMS and determine their applicable requirements.

## Interested Parties

| Interested Party | Relevant Requirements | Monitoring Method |
|------------------|----------------------|------------------|
| Customers | Product performance, accuracy, on-time delivery, regulatory compliance | Order review, customer feedback |
| Suppliers | Clear specifications, timely communication, payment terms | Supplier evaluation |
| Employees | Clear roles, training, safe work environment | Training records, meetings |
| Regulatory Bodies | Compliance with applicable regulations | Compliance review |
| Certification Body | Compliance with ISO 9001 requirements | Internal audits |
| Ownership / Management | Profitability, growth, reputation | Management review |

## Review Method

Interested parties and their requirements are reviewed during Management Review and updated as necessary.
