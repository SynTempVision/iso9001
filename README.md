# SynTemp Vision  
# ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System (QMS)

This repository contains the controlled Quality Management System (QMS) documentation for SynTemp Vision in accordance with ISO 9001:2015.

Only documents in the `main` branch are approved for use.  
Changes require review and approval prior to implementation.  
Printed copies are considered uncontrolled.  

Document control is maintained through Git version history.

---

## QMS Scope

The Quality Management System applies to the design, manufacture, assembly, testing, and distribution of temperature measurement instrumentation and engineered solutions for high-temperature and critical applications.

---

## Core QMS Documents

The following documents satisfy ISO 9001 documented requirements:

- `01_Scope.md`
- `02_Quality_Policy.md`
- `03_Quality_Objectives.md`

Additional records and evidence are maintained within the repository to support operational control and continual improvement.

---

## Operational Overview

SynTemp’s core process flow is:

Customer Order  
→ Work Order & Order Review  
→ Material Selection  
→ Manufacturing / Fabrication  
→ Assembly  
→ Testing / Verification  
→ Inspection  
→ Release & Shipment  

Quality controls are applied at defined verification and inspection stages to prevent the release of nonconforming product.

---

## Records and Evidence

The QMS is supported by controlled records including:

- Work Orders
- Inspection and Calibration Records
- Nonconformance Records
- Internal Audit Records
- Management Review Records
- Corrective Action Records
- Training Records

These records provide objective evidence of conformity and system effectiveness.

---

## Role Flexibility

Due to company size, individuals may perform multiple operational roles.  
Quality controls and documented records ensure proper segregation of review and approval activities where required.
