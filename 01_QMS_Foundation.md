## 4.3 Scope of the QMS

The Quality Management System of SynTemp applies to the design, manufacture, assembly, testing, and distribution of temperature measurement instrumentation and engineered solutions for high-temperature and critical applications.

All requirements of ISO 9001:2015 are applicable unless specifically justified.

---

## 4.1 Context of the Organization

SynTemp considers internal and external issues that may affect product conformity and customer satisfaction, including:

- Supply chain availability
- Regulatory requirements
- Customer specifications
- Personnel competence
- Equipment reliability
- Documentation control

These factors are reviewed during Management Review.

---

## 4.2 Interested Parties

Relevant interested parties include:

- Customers
- Suppliers
- Employees
- Regulatory bodies
- Certification body
- Ownership / Management

Their requirements are considered during order review, supplier control, and management review.

---

## 4.4 Core Process Flow

Customer Order  
→ Work Order  
→ Material Selection  
→ Manufacturing  
→ Assembly  
→ Testing / Verification  
→ Inspection  
→ Release & Shipment  

Supporting processes include document control, supplier control, corrective action, and management review.

Process performance is monitored and reviewed for effectiveness.

---

## 6.1 Risk and Opportunity

Risks and opportunities are identified through:

- Context review
- Customer requirements
- Supplier performance
- Nonconformance trends

Actions to address risks are implemented through operational controls and reviewed during Management Review.
