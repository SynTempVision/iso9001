## 2. External Issues

SynTemp evaluates and monitors external factors including:

- Availability and lead times of raw materials 
- Regulatory and compliance requirements applicable to temperature measurement instrumentation
- Customer-specific technical, contractual, and performance requirements
- Technological advancements in sensing, calibration, and measurement systems
- Market competition and industry standards
- Economic conditions impacting material cost and supply stability
- Cybersecurity and data integrity risks affecting operational systems

These factors are monitored through supplier communication, customer interaction, industry awareness, and management review.

---

## 3. Internal Issues

Internal factors considered include:

- Technical competence, training, and availability of personnel
- Manufacturing capacity and workload management
- Infrastructure reliability (equipment, tooling, calibration devices, software systems)
- Dependence on key suppliers or specialized materials
- Process control, documentation discipline, and record retention
- Equipment maintenance and calibration status
- Organizational growth, staffing changes, or restructuring

These factors are reviewed to ensure operational stability and continued conformity of products.
