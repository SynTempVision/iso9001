## Purpose

To define how Top Management demonstrates leadership and commitment to the Quality Management System (QMS).

---

## Leadership Commitment

Top Management at SynTemp demonstrates commitment to the QMS by:

- Taking accountability for the effectiveness of the QMS
- Ensuring the Quality Policy and Quality Objectives are established and aligned with the strategic direction of the organization
- Promoting the use of the process approach and risk-based thinking
- Ensuring that necessary resources are available
- Communicating the importance of meeting customer and regulatory requirements
- Supporting personnel in contributing to the effectiveness of the QMS
- Promoting continual improvement

---

## Customer Focus

Top Management ensures that:

- Customer requirements are determined and reviewed prior to acceptance
- Risks that could affect product conformity or customer satisfaction are addressed
- Customer satisfaction is monitored and evaluated

---

## Responsibility

Top Management is responsible for maintaining active involvement in the QMS through participation in Management Review, approval of policies and objectives, and oversight of corrective actions and improvements.
