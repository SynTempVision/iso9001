## Quality Policy

SynTemp is committed to providing high-quality temperature measurement instrumentation and engineered solutions that consistently meet customer requirements and applicable regulatory requirements.

We achieve this commitment by:

- Designing, manufacturing, assembling, testing, and distributing products in accordance with defined specifications
- Implementing and maintaining an effective Quality Management System based on ISO 9001
- Ensuring quality is built into our processes through contract review, verification, and control of nonconforming outputs
- Maintaining controlled documentation and records to ensure consistency and traceability
- Promoting competence, accountability, and quality awareness throughout the organization
- Continually improving our processes, products, and Quality Management System

Top Management is committed to supporting the Quality Management System, providing the necessary resources, and ensuring this policy is understood, implemented, and maintained throughout the organization.
