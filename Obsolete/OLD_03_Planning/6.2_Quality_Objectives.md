---

## Purpose

To establish measurable Quality Objectives that support the Quality Policy and ensure continual improvement of the Quality Management System (QMS).

---

## Quality Objectives

SynTemp establishes the following Quality Objectives:

### 1. On-Time Delivery
- Objective: ≥ 95% of orders delivered on or before committed date
- Measured By: Shipping records
- Responsible: 
- Reviewed: During Management Review

### 2. Product Conformity
- Objective: ≤ 2% internal nonconforming output rate
- Measured By: Nonconformance records
- Responsible: 
- Reviewed: During Management Review

### 3. Customer Satisfaction
- Objective: Minimize customer complaints and respond within 5 business days
- Measured By: Customer complaint log
- Responsible: 
- Reviewed: During Management Review

### 4. Internal Audit Completion
- Objective: 100% of planned internal audits completed annually
- Measured By: Audit schedule and reports
- Responsible:  
- Reviewed: Annually

---

## Planning to Achieve Objectives

To achieve these objectives, SynTemp:

- Implements controlled operational procedures
- Conducts order review and inspection activities
- Monitors nonconformance trends
- Reviews performance data during Management Review
- Implements corrective actions where necessary

---

## Monitoring and Review

Quality Objectives are:

- Monitored periodically
- Reviewed at least annually during Management Review
- Updated as necessary to support continual improvement

---

## Responsibility

Top Management ensures Quality Objectives are communicated and that necessary resources are available to achieve them.
