# 6.1 Actions to Address Risks and Opportunities

Version: 1.0

Approved By:   
Effective Date: 2026-03-01  

---

## Purpose

To identify risks and opportunities that may affect the Quality Management System (QMS) and to define actions necessary to address them.

---

## Risk Identification

SynTemp identifies risks and opportunities based on:

- Internal and external issues (Clause 4.1)
- Interested party requirements (Clause 4.2)
- Customer requirements
- Supplier performance
- Nonconformance trends
- Operational changes

Risks are evaluated based on their potential impact on product conformity, customer satisfaction, and business continuity.

---

## Typical Risk Areas

Examples of risks considered include:

- Supply chain delays or material shortages
- Incorrect or incomplete customer specifications
- Equipment malfunction or calibration lapse
- Human error during manufacturing or inspection
- Loss of key personnel
- Documentation control failures

---

## Actions to Address Risks

Actions may include:

- Approved supplier controls
- Order review process
- Equipment maintenance and calibration controls
- Training and competency programs
- Work order verification and inspection steps
- Internal audits and corrective actions

Actions are implemented through existing operational procedures and reviewed during Management Review.

---

## Opportunities

Opportunities for improvement may include:

- Process efficiency improvements
- Reduction of nonconforming outputs
- Improved supplier performance
- Enhanced training programs
- Expanded product capabilities

---

## Monitoring and Review

Risks and opportunities are reviewed:

- During Management Review
- When significant changes occur
- When trends indicate emerging issues

Effectiveness of actions is evaluated through performance monitoring, internal audit results, and corrective action outcomes.

---

## Responsibility

Top Management is responsible for ensuring that risks and opportunities are identified and addressed within the QMS.
