## Policy Statement

SynTemp is committed to providing high-quality temperature measurement instrumentation and engineered solutions that meet customer and applicable regulatory requirements.

We achieve this by:

- Operating in accordance with defined specifications
- Maintaining an effective Quality Management System based on ISO 9001
- Controlling nonconforming outputs
- Maintaining clear documentation and records
- Promoting competence and accountability
- Continually improving our processes and products

Top Management is responsible for ensuring this policy is implemented, understood, and maintained throughout the organization.
